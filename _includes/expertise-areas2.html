Creativity and Aesthetics

For me creativity is the ability to understand the root problem and to create a sollution which can be operated without complications. If the experience and funtionality of the designed system is integrated in all its parts, I would call it aestethics. (Norman, 2011; Isaacson, 2011)

Technology and Realization

For me technology is an imaginary reality of a homo sapien to enlarge its capabilities (Harari, 2017). Realization is the craftmenship to create this reality in a way other homo sapiens believe in this imaginary reality in the spiritual, mental or physical dimensions. (Sennett, 2008; Mensvoort, 2013)

User and Society

For me this is about understanding individuals interacting with others and systems by observations and engagement. Finding the root problem in someones life and satisfy their needs, interests, rights and wishes eventhough they did not know themeselves (Max-neef, et all, 1992; Governance and Integrity, 2008, Norman, 2011).

Math, Data and Computing

For me this area is all about data collection, analysis, visualisation, representation and modeling towards a more in-depth and enriche understanding of the root problem and context of the design topic by analog sketching and modeling and digital computer coding data-handling and visualisation. (Eindhoven University of Technology, 2019)

Business and Entrepreneurship

Economics in the transformation paradigm ask other principles like system thinking, network engagement and value sharing. These principles ask more responsible and social engaged behaviour from profesionals in both private and public organisations. For me this area is all about coalitions of collaborating organisations with different ethics, cultures and visions. The designer needs to navigate in an emerging ecosystem on different levels of society to create value for the involved people and organisations.
